The hiking club for those that go solo

If you're in to hiking through some of the UK's finest scenery, but prefer to do it alone, we're here to help you along the way to feel more confident in the outdoors. We'll provide simple routes and maps, the best gear reviews and if you're feeling brave, the odd community, no pressure meetups in amazing places.
